About Me

Hello! I am a third-year Ph.D. student at University of New South Wales (UNSW Sydney), supervised by Dr.Dong Gong and Dr.Alan Blair.

Before that, I obtained my Master's degree at Dalian University of Technology (DLUT) in 2024, supervised by Associate Prof. Yi Li and Prof. Yanqing Guo. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Dalian Maritime University.

My current research interest lies in Reinforcement Learning and Video World Models.
